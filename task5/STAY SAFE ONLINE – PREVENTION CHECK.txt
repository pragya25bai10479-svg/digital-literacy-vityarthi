STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

✓ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking passwords
✓ Remember: You do NOT need a UPI PIN to receive money
✓ Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources
✓ Always verify payment requests before approving
✓ Avoid clicking suspicious links in SMS or WhatsApp
✓ Do not install screen-sharing apps when asked by strangers
✓ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
✓ Log out from shared or public devices
✓ Keep your mobile and apps updated regularly
✓ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions

Financial / UPI Safety:
✓ Double-check receiver name before sending money
✓ Set daily transaction limits in your UPI app

Report Cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
