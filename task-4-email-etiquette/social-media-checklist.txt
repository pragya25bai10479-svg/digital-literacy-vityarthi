SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS

DO:
✓ Think before you post and consider long-term impact
✓ Keep your profile respectful and professional
✓ Verify information before sharing or reposting
✓ Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
✓ Be respectful and polite in comments and discussions

DON'T:
✗ Share personal details like phone number or address publicly
✗ Post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
✗ Spread rumors, fake news, or unverified information
✗ Engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
✗ Overshare your live location or daily routine
